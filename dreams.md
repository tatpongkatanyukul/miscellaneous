Curiosity
  * Turkey: bazaar (grand bazaar)
    * short trip: 8 days: Istanbul (3 days) - Cappadocia (5 days, inc. logistics)
  * Norway: viking (Viking Ship Museum, Oslo Medieval Festival, Vikingvalley, Tønsberg Vikingfestival, Nordvegen History Centre and Viking Settlement, St. Olav’s Way)
    * short trip: 7 days: Oslo (3 days) - Vikingvalley (Gudvangen) - Bergen (2 days) 
  * Russia: onion-dome church (Suzdal)
    * short trip: 9 days: Moscow (3 days) - Suzdal (2 days) - Karzan (4 days, inc. logistics)
  * Uzbekistan: silkroad heritage (Samarkand, Bukara)
    * short trip: 6 days: Samarkand (3 days) - Bukara (3 days)
  * Morocco: medina (Marrakesh, Fez), oasis (Tioute, Fint, Tinghir), fortified town (Ouarzazate, Ait Benhaddou), gateway to Sahara (Zagora, Merzouga); optional: Essaouira (port city), Taroudant (charming town)
    * short trip: 8 days: Marrakesh (2 days) - Ouarzazate - Ait Benhaddou - Fint oasis - Zagora (2 days) - Essaouira (2 days)
  * Arab: desert (e.g., Dubai desert conservation reserve, Wadi Rum), arab culture ([Centre for Cultural Understanding](https://www.cultures.ae/cultural-meals-public/), bedouin camp), mosque (Jumeirah, Al Farooq)
    * short trip: 4 days: Dubai desert conservation reserve - Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood (visit Centre for Cultural Understanding), Jumeirah mosque
  * India: hindu sites () 
    * south india: 8 days: Mysore (2 days) - Sri Chamundeshwari Ammanavara Temple (1 day)  - Brindavan Gardens (1 day) - Bylakuppe (Tibetan buddhist monastery) - Madikeri (hill station)
    * north india: 9 days: Varanasi (3 days) - Bodhgaya (6 days)
  * Africa: NPs, tribes (Masai village, !Khwa ttu San culture and education centre, Lesedi Cultural Village) 
    * Serengeti NP, Kruger NP, Lake Nakuru NP, Ngorongoro NP, Etosha NP, Amboseli NP, Lake Manyara NP, Virunga NP, Hwange NP, Namib-Naukluft NP
    * short trip: 5 days: Johannesburg (2 days) - Kruger NP (2 days) - Lesedi Cultural Village (1 day)
    * dream trip: 10 days: Serengeti NP - Ngorongoro NP - Lake Manyara NP
  * Azerbaijan: zoroastrian temple (Ateshgah of Baku), shi'ite mosque (Bibi-Heybat)
    * short trip: 3 days: Baku 
  * Borneo: NP, tribe
    * NPs: Bako NP, Gunung Mulu NP, Gunung Gading NP, Lambir Hills NP, Kubah NP, Niah NP, Kinabalu NP, Tawau Hills NP, Crocker Range NP, Ulu Temburong NP, Tanjung Puting NP, Sebangau NP
    * short trip: 7 days: Kuching (2 days) - Sarawak cultural village (1 day) - Semenggoh Wildlife Centre (1 day) - Bako NP (3 days)
  * Tahiti
